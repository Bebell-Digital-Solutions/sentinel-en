Manage & Disconnect Third-Party Apps From Gmail Account

This tutorial explains how to disconnect third-party applications connected to your Gmail account. If you use your Gmail account to sign into third-party applications, you will learn to manage and disconnect any unwanted application. This procedure is recommended for both Google Personal & Business Accounts.

This tutorial explains how to disconnect and unsubscribe third-party applications connected to your Gmail account. If you use your Google Account to sign into any web application follow these steps to manage and remove app permissions.

1 Go to www.google.com

Open your web browser and navigate to "Google.com", Google's main homepage. Make sure you are logged into your personal Google Account.

Important

Make sure you are logged into your "Google Account".

2 Open Google Account Menu

Click on your profile picture or initial in the top-right corner to access your Google account options menu.

3 Access Google Account Settings

Click "Manage your Google Account" button beneath your profile picture to open your account settings page where you can control all your Google services and permissions.

4 Navigate To Account Management

You will be redirected to the "myaccount.google.com" page where you can manage all your Google account details and settings.

5 Open Third-Party Connections

Select the "Third-party connections" option from the left-side menu or navigate to the section where you can view apps and services connected to your Google account.

6 Select Application to Disconnect

Select any app you don't recognize or want to disconnect. Click on the "Application" name to open and see the permissions and connections page for that specific app.

7 Delete Connections

Select "Delete all connections you have with [Application]" to remove its access to your Google account. This will revoke all permissions granted to this application.

8 Review Data Deletion Notice

Read any warnings about what data will be removed or affected by this action.

9 Confirm Action

Click on the "Confirm" button to complete the deletion process.

10 Completion & Next Steps

You have successfully unsubscribed the third-party app from your Gmail account by removing its access. To fully delete your data shared with this application, visit their website directly for further actions.

Important Note

Disconnecting an app from Google only removes its access to your account. To delete your data from the app's servers, you must contact the app developer directly or use their data deletion tools.
