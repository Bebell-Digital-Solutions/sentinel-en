How to Delete Your Personal Activity or Data From Google Securely

A comprehensive step-by-step guide to manage and remove your Google activity data effectively. This procedure is recommended for Google Personal Accounts.

This tutorial explains how to delete your personal data from your Google account. First, open your Chrome Browser or your preferred browser and search for Google.

1 Go to www.google.com

You will learn the necessary steps to manage and remove your activity data effectively. This procedure is recommended for "Google Personal Accounts"

2 Open Google Homepage

Open the Google homepage and click on your profile picture to begin managing your account settings.

N.B.

Make sure you are logged into your "Personal Google Account".

3 Access Google Account Settings

Click "Manage your Google Account" to open your account management page.

4 Navigate to Account Management

Switch to the myaccount.google.com page to access detailed account controls.

5 Open Account Search Feature

Click "Search Google Account" to find specific settings and options.

6 Enter Search Query

Enter your search query for "My Google Activity" to locate your activity data.

7 Select Data & Privacy Section

Click "My Activity" under the Data & privacy section to view your activity records.

8 Go to My Activity Page

Switch to myactivity.google.com to manage your Google activity data.

9 Open Activity Controls

Click the appropriate area to access your activity controls and settings.

10 Access Delete Options

Click the option that allows you to delete your activity data.

11 Review Deletion Steps

Click "3 steps" to review the process for deleting your Google activity data.

12 Proceed to Next Step

Click "Next" to continue with the data deletion process.

13 Confirm Deletion Access

Click the designated area to confirm your intent to delete data.

14 Initiate Data Deletion

Click "Delete" to remove your selected Google activity data.

15 Acknowledge Deletion Notice

Click "Got it" to acknowledge the confirmation message about data deletion.

16 Open Activity Settings

Click the relevant area to access your activity settings for further management.

17 Enable Activity Controls

Click "On" to enable activity controls that manage data collection.

18 Disable Activity Tracking

Click "Turn off" to stop Google from saving your activity data.

19 Access Confirmation Dialog

Click the area to open the confirmation dialog for turning off activity tracking.

20 Confirm Settings Change

Click "Got it" to confirm that activity tracking has been turned off.

21 Open Additional Options

Click the designated area to access more options related to your activity data.

22 Dismiss Notifications

Click "Dismiss" to close any notifications or alerts related to your activity data.

23 Access Time Range Settings

Click the area to open settings for selecting the time range of data to delete.

24 Select All Time Range

Click "All time" to choose the entire history of your Google activity data for deletion.

25 Confirm Data Deletion

Click "Delete" to permanently remove your Google activity data for the selected time range.

26 Acknowledge Completion

Click "Got it" to confirm that your data deletion process is complete.

27 Completion

You have successfully deleted your Google activity data and disabled activity tracking. To maintain your privacy, consider reviewing other Google account settings and managing data collection preferences regularly.
