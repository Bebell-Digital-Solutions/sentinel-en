How to Manage and Unsubscribe from Third-Party Applications in Gmail

A comprehensive step-by-step guide to review and remove third-party app permissions connected to your Gmail account. This procedure is recommended for Google Personal Accounts.

This tutorial explains how to manage and unsubscribe to Newsletters from all applications connected to your Gmail account.

1 Go to www.google.com

Open your Chrome browser or any preferred browser and go to Google.com.

2 Sign into Your Google Account

Make sure you are logged into your personal Google Account. Check the top-right corner for your profile picture.

N.B.

Make sure you are logged into your "Personal Google Account".

3 Open Gmail

From the Google home screen, click on the Gmail icon or go directly to Gmail.com to open the Gmail Application.

4 Navigate to Third-Party Apps

Once inside Gmail, navigate to the top search field. The URL should look like: https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#inbox

5 Modify the URL

Select "u/0/#inbox" in the URL and replace it with "#sub" then press "enter". The new URL should look like: https://mail.google.com/mail/#sub

6 Access Subscriptions Page

Press Enter. You will be taken to the subscriptions page where you can see, manage and unsubscribe from all the third-party apps you subscribed to manually.

7 Review Connected Apps

On this page, you'll see a list of all third-party applications with access to your Gmail account. Review each application carefully.

8 Remove App Access

To unsubscribe from an application, click on the "Unsubscribe" button on the right or click on it and select "Remove Access" or "Unsubscribe". Confirm the action when prompted.

10 Completion

You have successfully managed and unsubscribed from third-party applications in Gmail. Regularly review these permissions to maintain your account security and privacy.
