Inbox and Account Cleanup

Reduce your digital footprint by cleaning up old accounts and managing your inbox.

What this is

This section guides you through identifying and closing unused online accounts, unsubscribing from unwanted newsletters, and generally reducing the number of digital access points that could be exploited.

Plain definition

Minimizing your online presence by deactivating dormant accounts and decluttering your digital communications to reduce potential attack surfaces.

Why it matters

Every online account you have, especially those you no longer use, represents a potential vulnerability. If an old, forgotten account is breached, your associated email or other personal data could be exposed without your knowledge.

Real risk explanation with concrete examples

You might have signed up for a forum or an old e-commerce site years ago and forgotten about it. If that site suffers a data breach, your old username and password (which you might have reused) could be leaked. This provides attackers with information they can use to try and access your active accounts. Cleaning up these dormant accounts removes these hidden risks.
