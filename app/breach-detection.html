Breach Detection

Understand how to detect if your data has been exposed and what to do next.

What this is

This section focuses on tools and practices to monitor for potential data breaches involving your personal information and provides a clear plan of action if your data is found to be exposed.

Plain definition

Proactively checking if your email addresses, passwords, or other personal data have been compromised in public data breaches and knowing how to respond.

Why it matters

Data breaches are common, and your information might be exposed without your direct knowledge. Early detection allows you to take protective measures before attackers can exploit your compromised data.

Real risk explanation with concrete examples

If your email address and a password from an old forum are leaked in a breach, attackers might use that information to try and log into your other accounts. By using a breach detection service, you can be alerted to this exposure, allowing you to immediately change the compromised password and enable 2FA on affected accounts, preventing further damage.
